Features

Flow Simulation

Model your clinic's entire patient journey from check-in to checkout. Define stations, staff, shifts, and appointment types. See how patients move through your system.

🔍

Bottleneck Detection

Instantly see which station is causing delays, whether it's a capacity problem or a staffing problem, and exactly when during the day it's worst.

Scenario Comparison

Save your baseline, change one variable, re-run, and compare side by side. See the exact impact of adding a provider, staggering lunches, or adjusting your template.

Template Scheduling

Plug in your actual appointment template with specific times and provider assignments. See how your real schedule performs, not just a random simulation.

Swim Lane Flow Map

Visual timeline showing every patient's journey through each station. Hover to see individual wait and service times. Zoom into problem periods.

How It Works
From setup to insight in under 5 minutes.
Start with a preset or build your clinic from scratch.

Define your clinic

Set up your stations, staff roles, shift times, lunch breaks, and appointment types. Or start with a preset and customize.

Build your schedule

Use random arrivals for quick exploration, or enter your actual appointment template with specific provider assignments.

Run the simulation

The engine processes every patient through your clinic flow, respecting staff availability, room capacity, and lunch breaks.

Compare and decide

Questions Clinic Tempo Answers
The decisions you're already making — with better data.
Every one of these is a real question that practice managers deal with regularly.
👩‍⚕️

"Should I add a third provider on Tuesdays?"

Model your current 2-provider Tuesday, then add a third and compare. See exactly how much wait time drops and whether your support staff can handle the volume.

"Should I stagger staff lunches?"

Run synchronized vs. staggered lunch scenarios. See the wait time spike when everyone breaks at noon versus maintaining coverage.

Increase your template volume and see where the system breaks. Know your capacity ceiling before you hit it.

"How should I restructure when Dr. Smith goes part-time?"

Model the reduced provider hours and test different schedule layouts. Find the configuration that maintains access without burning out your staff.
